# User Flows

## Recruitment to Onboarding

1. Recruiter creates a job with configurable hiring workflow.
2. Job is published to career portal and integrations.
3. Candidates apply or are imported.
4. AI ranks resumes using tenant-approved criteria.
5. Interviews are scheduled and feedback is collected.
6. Offer is generated from a versioned template.
7. Accepted candidate becomes a prehire employee.
8. Onboarding workflow collects documents, forms, assets, and approvals.

## Payroll Run

1. Payroll admin selects legal entity, pay group, and cycle.
2. Engine resolves effective salary structures, attendance, leave, benefits, taxes, and compliance rules.
3. Configurable formulas and slabs are evaluated.
4. Exceptions are routed through workflow approval.
5. Payslips and compliance outputs are generated.
6. Audit trace is retained for every calculation.

## Employee Exit

1. Employee, manager, or HR initiates resignation, retirement, or termination.
2. Workflow assigns knowledge transfer, asset recovery, access removal, and interviews.
3. Payroll computes full and final settlement using configured rules.
4. Compliance documents are generated from tenant templates.
5. Employee record is retained according to data retention policy.
